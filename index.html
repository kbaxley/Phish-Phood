Lake Virginia Fishing Guide

Welcome to the Lake Virginia Ultimate Fishing Guide! The only resource you will need for fishing in Winter Park's beautiful Lake Virginia. Explore the site to discover all the species that inhabit our gorgeous lake, which bait works best for individual species, their preferred environmental conditions, and check out the current weather conditions for the lake. Visit our glossary page to research more about the fish in Lake Virginia or head over to our support page to find out about the Freshwater Alliance and how Rollins is pioneering research to keep our natural resources in tip-top shape!
