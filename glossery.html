Large Mouth Bass
Micropterus salmoides
Largemouth bass are a popular freshwater fish known for their aggressive behavior and large mouths.
Prefer calm or slow-moving freshwater bodies with abundant vegetation.
Preferred baits include minnows, worms, and soft plastics.
