About the Freshwater Alliance at Rollins

A collaborative initiative to engage the Central Florida community in preserving our freshwater resources.

The Freshwater Alliance at Rollins (FAR) integrates environmental science and humanities to explore the multifaceted challenges facing Central Florida's watershed. By collaborating with students, faculty, and community partners, FAR develops engaging projects and field experiences to inspire action and learning.

  • Interdisciplinary projects to bridge science and humanities.
  • Field research on water quality, aquatic life, and ecosystems.
  • Curriculum innovations to incorporate freshwater content.
  • Community outreach to promote responsible stewardship.

Our Mission

To foster a deeper understanding and appreciation for Florida's vital freshwater ecosystems.

Our Collaboration

The Ultimate Fishing Guide is proud to partner with the Freshwater Alliance at Rollins to promote awareness of Lake Virginia and its ecological significance. Our website provides valuable resources for understanding the biodiversity of Lake Virginia and the importance of preserving its natural beauty and health.

Through this collaboration, we aim to inspire the community to engage with their environment and adopt sustainable practices. Together, we strive to ensure that Lake Virginia remains a vibrant and thriving ecosystem for future generations.
