Fresh Water Alliance

The Freshwater Alliance at Rollins (FAR) is a collaborative program that engages the Central Florida community in transdisciplinary and liberal arts-based approaches to spark learning and action related to our freshwater resources and ecosystems.
Freshwater Alliance
Freshwater Alliance is important for raising awareness about the need to protect freshwater ecosystems, which are crucial for life and wildlife. By educating the public, these alliances help people understand why it's essential to take care of our water resources and the challenges that come with it. Their work encourages people to adopt sustainable practices, collaborate across different fields, and come up with community-driven solutions to keep our water clean and healthy for the future.

Ultimate Fishing Guide

The Ultimate Fishing Guide aims to create a website fishing guide for Lake Virginia to promote healthy conservation. We want to promote the goals of the Freshwater Alliance by encouraging people to get outside and enjoy Winter Parks freshwater resources.
The fish types in Lake Virginia are listed in our Glossary page and also our Main page. You can see the fish along with the fish's name, bait, habitat and all other attributes of the fish!
When you navigate to the Main page, there are color coded guides based on the daily weather, time of the year and attributes of the fish. Green represents higher likelihood of catching the food, blue represents moderate likelihood and red represents low likelihood to catch the said fish!
